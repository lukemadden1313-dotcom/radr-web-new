Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 20, 2026

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how Radr collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the Platform. By using Radr, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

Account Information

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Profile details

Activity & Planning Data

  • Workout plans you create
  • Scheduled activities
  • Participation history

Messaging Data

  • Direct messages
  • Group chat content
  • Shared links or media

Location Information

If enabled, we may collect approximate or precise location data to support coordination features such as maps and shared activity visibility.

Device & Usage Data

  • Device type
  • App interactions
  • Analytics information about how the Platform is used

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected data to:

  • Provide and improve the Platform
  • Enable social planning and coordination features
  • Facilitate messaging and group chats
  • Personalize user experience
  • Maintain safety and prevent misuse
  • Analyze usage to improve performance

4. Location Data

Location sharing is optional. If enabled, it allows you to:

  • Share activity locations with friends
  • View approximate locations for coordination

You can disable location permissions at any time through your device or app settings.

5. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal data. We may share information with:

  • Service providers who help operate the Platform
  • Analytics providers to improve functionality
  • Legal authorities when required by law

We may also share anonymized or aggregated data that does not identify you personally.

6. Messaging & User Content

Messages and shared content are stored to operate the Platform and ensure safety. We may review content when necessary to enforce our Terms or respond to reports of misconduct.

7. Data Storage & Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data. However, no system can be completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Your Rights & Choices

You can:

  • Access or update your profile information
  • Adjust privacy and location settings
  • Delete your account at any time

Depending on your location, you may also have rights to request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data.

9. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide the Platform and comply with legal obligations. When data is no longer required, we will delete or anonymize it where feasible.

10. Children's Privacy

Radr is not intended for children under the age required by applicable law. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children without appropriate consent.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. If significant changes are made, we will notify users within the app or by other appropriate means.
